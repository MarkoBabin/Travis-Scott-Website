LIFE OF TRAVIS SCOTT

Jacques Webster, also known as Travis Scott was born way back on April 30. 1991. He was born in Houston, Texas but he was raised in Missouri City. Travis started playing piano when he was only 4 years old and eventually, music changed his life. He then started making his own beats and producing songs as well as videos. He was recognised by T.I and Kanye West and they brought him to the studio in LA, where he signed to G.O.O.D. Music . To this day, travis has 2 well-known albums, 1 mixtape and, one EP and his third, long awaited album ASTROWORLD is on the way! Travis is currently living in Los Angeles with his wife Kiley Jenner and baby girl Stormi.
